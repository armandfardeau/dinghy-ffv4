## Chronology

1. Preparation:
   * Put on the clear sheet
   * Shock the hale low
2. Slaughter:
   * Slaughter and shock high
3. End of the run:
   * Raise the vertical stabilizer
   * Start counter-heeling
   * Trim from the edge
   * Choke the cuningham
   * Resume low pitch

{{< /columns >}}

Think of tightening the buoy like a Formula 1 driver: with too tight a turn entry, you lose speed.
